What exactly is Youth Advocacy?

Youth Advocacy is the idea of engaging youth to address issues in our world (issues such as poverty, global warming, climate change, social justice, and even voting. Despite these issues having a last impact on many, it is important to address Youth concerns as they are the future who will step up and advocate for change in our world. Involving the youth in such matters will only prepare them to become great decision makers, analytical thinkers, and problem solvers going forward.
